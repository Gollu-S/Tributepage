Biography

Dr.Vikram Ambalal Sarabhai was an Indian physicist and astronomer
who initiated space research and helped develop nuclear power in India.
He was honoured with Padma Bhushan in 1966 and the Padma Vibhushan
(posthumously) in 1972. He is internationally regarded as the Father of the
Indian Space Program. He was also a close friend of Homi Jehangir Bhabha,
considered the father of India's Nuclear program. He was also Chairman of
the Atomic Energy Commission. He along with other Ahmedabad-based
industrialists played a major role in the creation of the of
institutions and PRL was the first step in that direction.
Vikram Sarabhai served of PRL from 1966-1971.
